App Title: Personal Vacation Tracking App


This app allows users to track the following details about their vacations:

  • Vacation type
    • i.e. "cruise", "getaway", "all-inclusive", etc.
  • Destination
  • Hotel
  • Start and End date
  • Excursion name and date scheduled within the vacaiton date range.
